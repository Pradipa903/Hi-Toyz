About Us

Recognizing the passion and nostalgia that toys bring, our founders, Sarah and Mark Johnson, embarked on a mission to create a haven for toy enthusiasts of all ages. Sarah and Mark, both avid collectors themselves, had a deep appreciation for the sentimental value and joy that toys hold. They wanted to create a space where collectors could come together, share their passion, and find rare and sought-after items. With this vision in mind, HI-Toyz was born. Located in the heart of the city, HI-Toyz quickly gained a reputation for its extensive selection of toys, ranging from vintage classics to modern collectibles. The store became a treasure trove for collectors, featuring a diverse range of action figures, dolls, board games, model kits, and more. What set HI-Toyz apart was not just its impressive inventory but also the personalized experience it offered. Sarah and Mark took pride in cultivating a warm and welcoming environment, where collectors felt comfortable and valued. They often organized events and workshops, inviting local collectors and industry experts to share their knowledge and experiences. Word of mouth spread, and HI-Toyz became a hub for collectors in the community. It became a meeting place where friendships were formed, stories were shared, and lifelong passions were nurtured. The store's reputation as a trusted and reliable source for collectors grew, attracting enthusiasts from near and far. Over the years, HI-Toyz expanded its reach beyond the physical store. They established an online presence, allowing collectors from around the world to access their unique offerings. Through their website and social media platforms, they showcased their latest acquisitions, organized online auctions, and engaged with a wider community of collectors. In recognition of their contributions to the local collectors' scene, HI-Toyz has received numerous accolades, including "Best Toy Store" and "Community Favorite" awards. These acknowledgments further solidified their position as a beloved institution among collectors. As HI-Toyz celebrates its fourth anniversary, Sarah and Mark remain committed to their initial vision, constantly sourcing new and exciting products to cater to their customers' evolving tastes. They continue to foster a sense of belonging and community among collectors, making sure that everyone who walks through the doors of HI-Toyz leaves with a smile and a cherished toy. Whether you're a lifelong collector, a casual enthusiast, or someone discovering the joy of toys for the first time, HI-Toyz invites you to explore their world of wonders and make memories that will last a lifetime.
